CUSTOMER
RATING SYSTEM

    The Customer Rating System (eCRS) uses data drawn from a number of sources to assess a customer's 'Probability of Default' (PD) using a scale of 1 to 23 (and 98 or 99 for defaulted assets). eCRS ratings must not be disclosed to any person or entity outside the Group.
    Bankers must complete a customer risk rating using eCRS for all customers that are excluded from behavioural scored customer ratings
